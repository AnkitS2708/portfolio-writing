About Me

Hi, I’m Ankit Singh from Patna, Bihar, a B.Tech student in Computer Science Engineering (CSE) at Dr. B.R. Ambedkar National Institute Of Technology Jalandhar (NITJ), currently in my 2nd year. Apart from my passion for technology, I am actively involved in Bawre Theatre and Rajbhasha Samiti, where I explore my love for dramatics and language. I’m also a writer, and I enjoy expressing ideas through storytelling, whether it's in the form of stage plays, street plays, short films, monoacts and many more. Balancing technology and creativity is something I truly enjoy, and I always look for ways to bring both worlds together!
